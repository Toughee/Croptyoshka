    Croptyoshka

    Croptyoshka is an open source, image editing software designed to make your work flow simpler and easier..

    How to use it

    Just drag the image into the window and change it to what you wish. You can also edit your images with effect presets. Whether you're a designer, photographer, copywriter, web developer, or a just a regular web user, Croptyoshka is guaranteed to help you out immenesly.
